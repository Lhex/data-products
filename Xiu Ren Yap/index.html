Diamond Price Predictor

by carat

Xiu Ren Yap

Aim

This app makes a quick estimate of the price of a diamond based on the carat, so that one can make an informed choice before buying or selling a diamond.

Method

In our app, we use a linear model to fit through the data from the dataset of diamonds. Therefore, the prediction is but a very rough estimate.

library(ggplot2)
data(diamonds)
modelFit <- lm(price ~ carat, data = diamonds)

Output

From the model fit shown on the slide before, we estimate the price as below. Let's look at an example where user enters 2 carat.

carat <- 2
perCarat <- coef(modelFit)[2]
intercept <- modelFit$coefficients[1]
totalPrice <- as.numeric(intercept + carat * perCarat)
totalPrice

## [1] 13256.49

where carat is the value obtained from the input from user.
